About Us

Your Trusted International Freight Forwarding Partner

Established in 2001, PT. LMT LOGISTICS is a privately owned international freight forwarding company equipped with high-standard cargo handling qualifications. With a commitment to operational excellence, we provide integrated logistics solutions that ensure reliability, cost efficiency, and on-time performance.

Our dedicated team, advanced systems, and strong global network enable us to deliver seamless services across air, sea, and land transportation. As a trusted partner, we prioritize the safety of every shipment and strive to exceed customer expectations through continuous improvement and professional service. More...

Vision:

We care about your cost, time, and safety in every shipment.

Mission:

  1. To provide efficient, affordable, and secure shipment services through reliable processes and continuous improvement.
  2. To deliver shipments on time, reduce customer costs, and ensure the highest safety standards in every delivery.
  3. To optimize logistics operations by using technology, trained personnel, and clear communication to protect customer time, budget, and cargo safety.
  4. To build customer trust by offering transparent pricing, fast service, and safe handling of all shipments.
  5. To support customers with responsive service, cost-effective solutions, and end-end shipment protection.
